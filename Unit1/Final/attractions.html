Attractions

Must-See Places You Can't Miss in Spain!'

1.La Sagrada Familia, Barcelona

- A stunning, unfinished basilica designed by the renowned Catalan architect Antoni Gaudí.

- Its unique Modernista architecture and ongoing construction make it an iconic landmark.

- The visiting hours have slight variations, what remains constant is that at any time of year and any day, the visiting hours of the Sagrada Familia are between 10:30 AM and 6:00 PM.

2.The Alhambra, Granada

- A vast complex of palaces and gardens that served as a fortress and royal residence for the Nasrid dynasty.

- It's a beautiful example of Moorish architecture, showcasing detailed designs and peaceful courtyards like the famous Court of the Lions.

- The palace is open from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM throughout the week, but is opened again from 8:00 to 9:30 PM on Fridays' and Saturdays'

3.The Great Mosque of Córdoba (Mezquita)

- A historic mosque, later converted into a cathedral, that demonstrates the remarkable blend of Islamic and Christian architectural influences.

- It's famous for its forest of horseshoe arches and columns which creates a unique and awe-inspiring atmosphere.

- The visiting hours vary depending on which time of the year you are visiting.

4.Seville's Royal Alcázar

- The Alcázar is a historic royal palace in Seville, Spain and one of the official residences of the Spanish royal family.

- The Alcázar's Mudéjar architecture reflects centuries of history, and the Cathedral houses Christopher Columbus's tomb, offering a glimpse into Spain's royal and religious past.

- The Royal Alcázar of Seville is open daily from 9:30 AM, with closing times varying by season.

5.Prado Museum

- The Museo Nacional del Prado, or Prado Museum, is the main Spanish national art museum located in Madrid, Spain, housing one of the world's finest collections of European art.

- Its vast collection includes over 8,000 paintings, 7,000 works of art, and 1,000 sculptures, with works by artists such as Titian and El Greco.

- The Museo del Prado is typically open Monday–Saturday from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. and on Sundays from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.
